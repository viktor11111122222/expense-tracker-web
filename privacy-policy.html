2 What Data We Collect

2a. App — Without an Account App

All core features of Money Tracker (expense logging, budgets, charts, categories, currencies) work entirely offline and locally on your device. No account is required and no personal data is sent to our servers for these features.

Data stored locally on your device includes:

  • Expense entries (amounts, categories, dates, notes, tags)
  • Income and budget settings
  • App preferences (language, currency, theme)
  • Recurring expense rules
  • Bill split calculations

This data never leaves your device unless you use the CSV export feature or the optional Shared Budget feature described below.

2b. App — Shared / Family Budget Feature (Account Required) App

When you choose to use the Shared Wallet or Family Budget feature, an account is required. Creating an account involves submitting:

  • Name — your display name
  • Email address — used for login and invite emails
  • Password — stored as a one-way bcrypt hash; we cannot read your password

Once logged in, shared expense data (amounts, categories, dates, notes) added to a shared wallet is stored on our server so that all wallet members can view it.

We also collect standard server-side information for security purposes:

  • IP address — used for rate-limiting and abuse prevention
  • Request timestamps and log entries — retained in server logs for operational purposes
  • JWT authentication tokens — expire after 7 days
If you invite someone to a shared wallet, you will provide their name and email address. By doing so, you confirm that you have their permission to share this information with us for the purpose of sending a single invitation email.

2c. Advertising Data (Google AdMob) App

Money Tracker may display ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob automatically collects certain data from your device to serve relevant ads. This may include:

  • Advertising ID (GAID on Android / IDFA on iOS)
  • Device model, operating system version
  • General location (country / region level)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)
  • App usage signals

This data is collected and processed by Google LLC under their own privacy policy. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings (Android: Settings → Privacy → Ads; iOS: Settings → Privacy → Tracking).

5b. App Local Storage App

The app stores all your financial data locally on your device using the browser's localStorage and the Capacitor Filesystem API (for CSV files). This data is private to the app on your device and is not accessible by other apps. It is removed when you uninstall the app or clear the app's data.

For the Shared Budget feature, the authentication token (JWT) is also stored locally in localStorage and expires after 7 days.

6 Data Security Both

We take the security of your data seriously and apply industry-standard measures to protect it. These include:

  • bcrypt password hashing — passwords are never stored in plain text; we cannot read them
  • JWT authentication — short-lived signed tokens (7-day expiry) for API access
  • HTTPS everywhere — all data in transit is encrypted using TLS
  • Rate limiting — authentication endpoints are rate-limited to prevent brute-force attacks
  • CORS restrictions — the API only accepts requests from known app origins
  • Minimal data collection — we only collect data that is strictly necessary for the feature to work

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security. If you suspect your account has been compromised, please contact us immediately.
